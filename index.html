Our Story

From Seed to Golden Oil

Das Oil and Agro Product is a focused agro-processing business built on one promise — delivering genuine, unadulterated mustard oil and its by-products to buyers who value purity.

We procure raw mustard seeds directly from farmers in bulk, process them through our Screw Oil Press / Expeller extraction unit, and supply the resulting oil and oil cake to bulk trade buyers and retail packaged markets across Tier 3 cities.

Our extraction unit is also open to suppliers and individuals who wish to bring their own seeds — mustard, groundnut, or other oilseeds — and have oil pressed on demand.
